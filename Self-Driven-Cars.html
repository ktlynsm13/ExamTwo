Pros

There are multiple Pros to having and using a self driving car. They have great potential and they are showing great advances in the technical world. A few of those pros are:

  • Increased Safety, most driving errors are due to human error, taking the human out of the problem solves it.
  • Reduced CO2 Emissions, since vehicles don't need to park in the city ther is more space for green.
  • Improve Traffic Flow, autonomous vehicles can talk with one another and corrdinate to help with traffic.

Cons

While there are multiple Pros for self drivings cars there are also multiple Cons. There are a number of dangers that come with self driving cars. Here are a few examples of the cons for self driving cars:

  • Expensive, costs go up with the technology
  • Surveillance, a car with software that runs all the time has the potential to get hacked.
  • Mixed Traffic, even though it can improve traffic it can also cause issue since not everyone is going to want a self driving car.
  • Job Loss, this could be an advantage for a business but this is a disadvantgae for individuals who rely on driving for their jobs.

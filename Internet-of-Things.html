Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things is ever growing circle of devices that connect to the internet in some way or form.

In recent years there has been multiple things added to the IoT and some of these things are the stuff that we use everyday that we just don't think about.

One of the biggest advancements of the Internet of Things is Bluetooth. There are so many things are that getting developed and created that are using Bluetooth in someway. Phones and cars are a major point for this, headphones connect to our phones via bluetoot and our phones will connect to our cars using it as well.

There are endless possibilities with the IoT, like it said its continously growing. The future for it is so widespread no one can really predict how it will be. Technology advanaces everyday which changes the IoT.
